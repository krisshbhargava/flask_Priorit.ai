Form Information

Fill out the following form for any maintenance requests and issues you may have in your dorm room/apartment. You may also attach image and media links, if necessary, in the description box.

Please use this form to report any issues in the following categories:

  • Kitchen & Bath
  • Safety & Electrical
    • Includes any issues with fire alarms, mold, HVAC unit.
  • Pest Control
  • General Maintenance
    • This includes key/lock issues, wall damage, and bed lofting.
  • Heating and Cooling

Note: Please put some detail into your maintenance issue description, as we will run your description through our algorithm, which will add your request to a priority queue based on our estimated urgency of your issue. If your maintenance request lacks sufficient information for our system to categorize it, we may reach out to you for clarifying information or images.

For emergency maintenance cases, please fill out this form and call +1 234-567-8910 immediately.
