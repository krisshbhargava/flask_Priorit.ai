Maintenance Request Form

Information

Fill out the following form for any maintenance requests and issues you may have in your dorm room/apartment.

Please use this form to report any issues in the following categories:

  • Kitchen & Bath
  • Safety & Electrical
    • Includes any issues with fire alarms, mold, HVAC unit.
  • Pest Control
  • General Maintenance
    • This includes key/lock issues, wall damage, and bed lofting.
  • Heating and Cooling

Please put some detail into your maintenance issue description, as we will run your description through our algorithm, which will add your request to a priority queue based on our estimated urgency of your issue.

For emergency maintenance cases, please fill out this form and call +1 234-567-8910 immediately.
